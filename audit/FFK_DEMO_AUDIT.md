## Headline corrections (critical, must fix)

1. **Captain is Amir Rrahmani**, not Muriqi.
2. **Squad value is ~€131m** (Transfermarkt, Nov 2025), not €42m. We undersell by 3×.

4. **FIFA ranking is 78** (April 2026), not 92. Real trajectory: 99 → 80 in 2025 (+19, biggest single-year jump of any nation that year, FIFA-confirmed +89.02 ranking points). Strong narrative we're missing.
5. **Vedat Muriqi**: 32 y/o (not 31), shirt #7 (not #9), 68 caps / 32 goals (not 53/26), debut 9 Oct 2016 vs Ukraine (not 24.03.2016 vs Faroe Islands), Mallorca contract through **30.06.2029** (not 2027), and **leading La Liga goalscorer 2025/26 with 21 goals — 2nd in Pichichi behind Mbappé**, broke Eto'o's RCD Mallorca **La Liga** scoring record on 12 April 2026 vs Rayo Vallecano (his brace took him to 55 La Liga Mallorca goals, surpassing Eto'o's 54). Single best line in the deck and we don't have it.
6. **EURO 2028 next-fixture countdown is fabricated.** Qualifying draw is 6 December 2026 in Belfast; matches start 26-27 March 2027. The "Kosovo vs Slovenia · 8 Sep 2026 · Group G" countdown card cannot exist.
7. **Edon Zhegrova is at Juventus**, not Lille (per Agent 2 peer-benchmarking source — flagged for double-check).
8. **Atdhe Nuhiu retired from Kosovo NT in 2021.** Remove everywhere.
9. **Hekuran Kryeziu retired (July 2023) and is now Kosovo U21 head coach.** Remove from squad lists.
10. **Veldin Hodža is 23 (not 26) and a defensive midfielder (not a striker).** Plays at Rubin Kazan (not Sturm Graz). FIFA approved his switch from Croatia youth on 2 Sep 2025. The "natural #9 successor to Muriqi" framing must be reassigned to Fisnik Asllani.
