URGENT · 6 months
Full-back depth
Three starters over 30 (Vojvoda 31, Hadergjonaj 31, Aliti 32). Dellova (27) is the rising option. No confirmed U21. Recommendation: focused scouting on the 2005–2007 cohort (Parduzi already in the watchlist).
02
WATCH · 12 months
Goalkeeper pipeline
Three viable senior options: Muric (27, Ipswich), Bekaj (28, KF Tirana), Saipi (25, FC Lugano). The challenge is U21 — no confirmed keeper in the next generation. Recommendation: assessment of U17/U19 keepers by March 2027.
03
PLAN · September 2026
#9 succession
Asllani (23, Hoffenheim) is the natural successor to Muriqi (32) — he started alongside Muriqi in the play-off final v. Türkiye. ~10 Bundesliga goals in 2025/26. Competition: A. Rrahmani 25, Zabërgja 25. Tactical decision: minutes in the autumn camps.
04
LONG-TERM · 24 months+
Centre midfield 2030+
Berisha (33) winding down. Rexhbeçaj (28) returning to Wolfsburg from 2026/27. Frokaj (21, U21 captain) is the closest prospect. The 2008–2009 cohort is thin in our watchlist — without action, the gap surfaces around 2031–2033.
